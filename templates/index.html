Transform The Energy Industry With Machine Learning


The extraction of energy from renewable sources is rapidly growing. The current pace of technological development makes it commercially viable to harness energy from sun, wind, geothermal and many other renewable sources. Renewable energy has the potential to provide 80 percent of U.S. electricity by 2050.


An inherent characteristic common to all renewable power plants is that power generation is dependent on environmental parameters and thus cannot be fully controlled or planned for in advance.

Machine learning techniques have been successfully used in the planning of renewable energy plants based on available data with reasonable accuracy. Researches on location, sizing, and configuration of renewable energy based on machine learning techniques underline it's popularity, particularly in isolated areas. This shows the importance of potential machine learning as a design tool in strategic planning and policy making for renewable energy.

A stronger reliance on machine learning algorithms can also save the customer’s money while rescuing the planet at the same time.
